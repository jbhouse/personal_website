The Origins of Athletics

Wrestling, the worlds oldest sport, dating back to the Romans and the original Olympic games. So does that make Greco-Roman (greco for short) wrestling; the oldest style of the oldest sport in the world, also the MANLIEST sport in the world?

Pictured above is a Greco wrestler performing a suplex, a move in which one wrestler lifts their opponent off their feet, throws them over their own head, and lands their opponent on their back or in any other "danger" position. (neck etc) a throw such as this is awarded 5 points and is the highest scoring move in greco wrestling, often ending the match.
